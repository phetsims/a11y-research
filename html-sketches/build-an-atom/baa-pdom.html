Build an Atom

Scene Summary

This sim has three screens, Atom, Symbol, and Game. Each sim screen contains a Play Area and a Control Panel.

  • On current screen the Play Area contains a model of an atom called My Atom, three buckets of particles, and three detail panels that track important things about My Atom. The Control Panel has radio buttons to switch the model, and checkboxes to show or hide labels in My Atom.
  • Look for particle buckets to play.
  • If needed, check out the keyboard shortcuts for this sim.
