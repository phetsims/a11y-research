Ohms Law

Scene Summary

In this sim, the Play Area contains the Ohm's Law equation, V equals I times R, a physical circuit, and sliders to play with voltage and resistance. The circuit wire connects a series of batteries and runs through a resistor. Batteries show amount of voltage, and dots in the resistor show the level of resistance in the resistor. In the Control Panel, use buttons to reset sim or mute sound.
