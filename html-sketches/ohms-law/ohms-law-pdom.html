Ohm's Law Equation

Voltage, V, is equal to Current, I, times Resistance, R.

With [[4.5]] volts, the size of V is [[large]] [[much larger than]] I with [[9.0]] millamps and [[comparable to]] the size of R with [[500]] ohms.
