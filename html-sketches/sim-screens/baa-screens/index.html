Atom Screen, Build an Atom

The interactive {{Atom Screen}} changes as you play with it. It has a Play Area and a Control Area. The Play Area {{has a model of an atom called My Atom, three buckets of particles, and three detail panels that track important things about the atom}}. The Control Area has {{radio buttons to switch the Atom model, checkboxes to adjust what inforamtion is shown,}} and a reset button to reset the sim.

Look for particle buckets to play.
